## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will auto-fix break my website?

**A:** No! SafeOrbit only auto-fixes **safe changes** that can't break your site. For anything risky (like updating plugins), we:
1. Create a backup first
2. Show you what will change
3. Ask for your approval
4. Give you a "rollback" button if something goes wrong

---

### Q: How often should I run optimization?

**A:** We recommend:
- **Monthly**: Run a full scan
- **Weekly**: Check for critical issues
- **After changes**: Scan after adding new plugins or changing themes

SafeOrbit can do this automatically and email you if it finds problems.

---

### Q: Do I need technical knowledge?
